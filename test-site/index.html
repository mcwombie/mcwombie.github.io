Things Relating to A Course in Miracles

November 2020

This page contains reflections, links and material related to my own personal experience as a student and teacher of A Course in Miracles, which I began incorporating into my life in 2017. Since that time I have gone through three workbook and text cycles, been a member of various Facebook study groups and explored a few of the spinoff books and podcasts by other students/teachers including:

  • Disappearance of the Universe by Gary Renard
  • Take Me to Truth by Nouk Sanchez and Tomas Vieira
  • A Crash Course in Miracles by Philip Urso

Presently I am reading The Meaning of Forgiveness by Kenneth Wapnick.

Here is a link to A Crash Course in Miracles Podcast which I highly recommend. It is my intention to continue daily exercises in the workbook next year as each re-read unveils new and deeper meaning.
